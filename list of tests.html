4 Best Afghan Main Courses

Qabili Plaw

Qabili plaw is the Afghan national dish. Unmistakable in its appearance with its glistening carrots and large tender chunks of lamb, Qabili plaw is an unforgettable dish. It is considered a festive and important dish due to the price and quality of the ingredients as well as its tradition of being Afghanistanâ€™s national cuisine. The dish is traditionally placed in the center of the meal with other foods making up the rest of the perimeter.

Bolani

Afghan Bolani are delicious potato-stuffed flatbreads made with homemade dough filled with mashed potatoes, green onions, cilantro, and green peppers. They are shallow-fried until crispy and golden brown, making them a perfect appetizer or snack packed with flavor.

Manto

Traditional Afghan steamed dumplings filled with seasoned ground meat, topped with a savory tomato sauce and yogurt. A soft, flavorful dish that reflects the warmth and hospitality of Afghan cuisine.

Kichiri Quroot

A flavorful Afghan classic made with rice, lentils, and Quroot, a tangy yogurt-based sauce. This dish is perfect for those looking to enjoy a comforting, hearty meal with a unique balance of savory and sour flavors. Ideal for all occasions!
