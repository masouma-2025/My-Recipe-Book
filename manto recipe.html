Mantu is one of the most popular Afghan food and it is common in gatherings and ceremonies. Also, you can find it in many Afghan restaurants. Mantu is an Afghan ravioli filled with beef and covered with yogurt sauce and tomato sauce. Therefore, this food that some people call manto or mantoo is a healthy and low-calorie appetizer. How to cook it?

The Mantu recipe has four steps. First, we cook the filling and the tomato sauce. Then we will wrap the ravioli and cook them with steam. Finally, we will make the yogurt sauce.

filling

  1. Chop two medium-sized onions into small pieces and heat them on low heat with four tablespoons of oil till making them soft.
  2. Add 300 gr minced beef to the onion and stir them well.
  3. Add 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, 1 tsp red chili, 1 tsp coriander, 1 tbsp salt, and 1 tsp turmeric to the meat and mix them well.
  4. Pour a cup of water. Cover the pan and let it cook for about 30 minutes. Our filling for Mantu is ready!

How to wrap the ravioli?

  1. Put one tablespoon of the filling in the center of a wonton wrapper.
  2. Dip your finger into water and moisture the corner of the wrapper.
  3. Overlay the opposite corners on each other and press them. Then grab the existing corners and press them. Seal the edges on each other.
  4. Repeat this step and wrap all of the dumplings.
